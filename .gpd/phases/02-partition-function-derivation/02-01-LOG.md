## Intermediate Results

### Key Equations Derived

1. **State space:** $s_i \in \{1, 2, \ldots, q\}$ (categorical)

2. **Hamiltonian:** $H = -J \sum_{\langle ij \rangle} \delta(s_i, s_j)$

3. **Mean-field approximation:** $\sum_{\langle ij \rangle} \rightarrow \frac{N}{2} \sum_{i \neq j}$

4. **Boltzmann factor:** $P(\{s_i\}) = \frac{e^{-\beta H}}{Z}$ with $\beta = 1/T_{\text{eff}}$

5. **Free energy:** $F = U - TS$
